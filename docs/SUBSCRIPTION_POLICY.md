# Subscription Policy

## Trial
- Length: 30 days from activation.
- One trial per user.
- Trial grants same entitlements as paid plans.

## Renewal
- Monthly: renews every 30 days.
- Yearly: renews every 12 months.
- Auto-renew enabled by default unless canceled.

## Cancellation
- Cancel at any time.
- Access continues until end of current billing period.
- Trial cancellation ends trial immediately and downgrades to Free.

## Access Policy
- Paid subscribers get full access to all app features.
- Free users see a paywall on the primary gated action (apply to opportunities).
- Optional secondary gates can be enabled (bookmarks or mentorship).

## Grace Period
- Payment failures trigger a 7-day grace period.
- Grace period keeps entitlements active.
- If unpaid after grace, downgrade to Free.

## Refunds
- Refund requests handled per store policy.
- Mark subscription as refunded and revoke paid entitlements if required.

## Billing Portal
- Users can update payment method, view invoices, and cancel.

## Compliance
- Use App Store and Play Store IAP for digital subscriptions.
- Surface terms, price, and renewal info before purchase.
