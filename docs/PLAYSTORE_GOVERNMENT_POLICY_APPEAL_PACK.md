# Play Store Government Affiliation Remediation Pack

Last updated: February 23, 2026

## 1) Suggested Short Description

Independent diaspora app for Rwanda news, opportunities, and community resources.

## 2) Suggested Full Description (Play Console)

Rwanda Connect is an independent, privately owned platform for the Rwandan diaspora.

Disclaimer: Rwanda Connect does not represent, is not affiliated with, and is not endorsed by the Government of Rwanda or any embassy/consulate.

What you can do in the app:
- Read curated news summaries from third-party publishers
- Discover opportunities (jobs, education, business, public notices)
- Save items and receive personalized alerts
- Access community resources and practical guides

Important:
- Rwanda Connect does not provide government services.
- Rwanda Connect does not process official applications on behalf of any government entity.
- For official requirements, procedures, deadlines, and legal decisions, users must verify directly with official government sources listed below.

Official public sources used for verification:
- Government of Rwanda Portal: https://www.gov.rw
- Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation (MINAFFET): https://www.minaffet.gov.rw
- Rwanda Development Board (RDB): https://rdb.rw
- Rwanda Revenue Authority (RRA): https://www.rra.gov.rw
- Rwanda Public Procurement Authority (RPPA): https://www.rppa.gov.rw
- Irembo e-Government Portal: https://www.irembo.gov.rw
- Rwanda Directorate General of Immigration and Emigration: https://www.migration.gov.rw

If any information differs between Rwanda Connect and an official source, the official source takes precedence.

## 3) Appeal Text Template (Play Console)

Subject: Policy Clarification and Metadata Update - Government Affiliation

Hello Google Play Review Team,

Thank you for your feedback. We understand the concern regarding potential government affiliation representation.

We confirm that Rwanda Connect is an independent private platform and is not a government entity, and not affiliated with or endorsed by the Government of Rwanda or any embassy.

To resolve this:
1. We updated our app listing description to include a clear and prominent non-government disclaimer.
2. We added clear official source links for government-related information referenced by the app.
3. We reviewed and adjusted in-app legal/disclaimer surfaces to reinforce that users must verify official information directly from official government websites.
4. We ensured the app does not claim to provide official government services or represent a government authority.

Please review the updated metadata and app content in the latest submission.

Thank you.

Regards,
Rwanda Connect Team

## 4) Pre-Submission Checklist

- Update Play Console short and full description with the text above.
- Remove or soften wording that implies official facilitation, such as:
  - "embassy content network"
  - "government services portal"
  - "official policy guidance" (unless clearly attributed and disclaimed)
- Ensure the disclaimer appears early in full description (first 2-3 lines).
- Keep official source URLs visible and valid in the full description.
- Upload new build if in-app disclaimer/legal text changed.
- In appeal, reference that metadata has been updated and include version code.

## 5) Optional Tightened Feature Wording

Prefer:
- "public notices and opportunities from third-party and public sources"
- "guides and educational content"
- "links out to original publishers and official portals"

Avoid:
- "facilitates government services"
- "official government updates"
- "embassy-backed network" (unless documented authorization is provided to Google)
