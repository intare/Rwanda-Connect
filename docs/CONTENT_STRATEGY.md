# Content Strategy

## Voice and Tone
- Professional, supportive, and clear.
- Avoid jargon; use short, direct sentences.

## Content Types
- News: short summaries with source attribution.
- Opportunities: verified listing with clear apply CTA.
- Events: location, time, and organizer visibility.
- Community: respectful, high-signal discussions.

## Empty States
- News: "No updates yet. Check back soon."
- Opportunities: "No results. Try changing filters."
- Events: "No events near you. Expand location filters."
- Community: "Start the first discussion."

## Error Messaging
- Auth: "Email or password is incorrect."
- Network: "Connection lost. Please retry."
- Form: "Please fill in all required fields."

## Legal and Compliance
- Add Terms of Service and Privacy Policy links.
- Show disclaimer for property and investment listings.
- Display source attribution for news and opportunities.

## Subscription Messaging
- Paywall headline: "Unlock full access to Rwanda Connect"
- Paywall subtext: "Apply to opportunities, save resources, and connect with mentors."
- Trial CTA: "Start free trial"
- Paid CTA: "Subscribe now"
- Cancellation note: "Cancel anytime before renewal."

## Billing Error Copy
- "Payment failed. Please try again or use a different method."
- "Your card was declined. Update your payment method."
- "We could not verify your payment. Please contact support."

## Upgrade Success Toasts
- "You're all set. Your subscription is active."
- "Welcome to Rwanda Connect Premium."

## Downgrade Messaging
- "Your subscription has ended. You're now on the Free plan."
- "Upgrade anytime to unlock full access."

## Trial Expiration Messaging
- "Your free trial has ended. Subscribe to keep full access."
- "Continue with the Free plan or upgrade to Premium."

## Push Notifications
- New opportunity: "New opportunity: {title} in {location}."
- Event reminder: "Reminder: {eventTitle} starts in 24 hours."
- Subscription renewal: "Your subscription renews tomorrow."
- Trial ending: "Your trial ends in 3 days. Subscribe to keep full access."

## Email Templates
- Welcome: Subject "Welcome to Rwanda Connect" Body "Thanks for joining. Complete your profile to get tailored updates."
- Trial start: Subject "Your free trial has started" Body "Enjoy full access. Cancel anytime before renewal."
- Payment failed: Subject "Payment failed" Body "Please update your payment method to keep access."
- Event RSVP: Subject "You're registered for {eventTitle}" Body "See event details and add to calendar."

## In-App Banners
- Trial ending: "Your trial ends in {days} days. Subscribe to keep full access."
- Payment failed: "Payment issue detected. Update your billing details."
- New features: "New: Mentorship is now available."
